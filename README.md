# solstone

an AI co-brain that experiences your life with you and gives you superhuman memory.

solstone is a system you have, in two parts you own. **observers** experience your day
along with you. a **journal** holds everything. **sol**, your keeper, lives in your
journal and curates your memories there. from that emerges a knowledge graph of every
person and project in your life, proactive meeting prep, automatic commitment tracking,
and full-text search across your whole digital life. you stop managing your memory and
start being present.

open source. self-host it, or let [sol pbc](https://solpbc.org) host it for you.
