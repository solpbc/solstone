🛟 Getting Help

Just say "I need help" or "something's not working" in the chat bar. Your sol will handle everything — searching for answers, running diagnostics, and filing a ticket if needed.

🔍 Search the Knowledge Base

Run sol call support search "your question" to find answers in our knowledge base before filing a ticket.

🩺 Run Diagnostics

Run sol call support diagnose to check your system health locally — no data is sent anywhere.

📢 Announcements

Run sol call support announcements to check for product updates and known issues.

🔒 Privacy

Nothing leaves your device without your explicit approval. You review every ticket before it's sent and can edit or redact anything. Journal content is never included unless you attach it yourself.
